GOA

A 3-Day Trip to Goa

Day 1: Arrival and Beach Exploration

On your first day, arrive at Goa and check into your hotel. After freshening up, head to the nearest beach and spend some time relaxing on the sand or taking a dip in the sea. In the evening, head to the famous Anjuna Flea Market, which is open only on Wednesdays, and shop for local souvenirs and handicrafts. You can also try some street food and enjoy live music performances.

Day 2: Local Sightseeing and Adventure Activities

On your second day, explore the local attractions of Goa. Visit the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Old Goa, which is known for its ancient churches and cathedrals. You can also visit the Basilica of Bom Jesus, which is home to the mortal remains of St. Francis Xavier. In the afternoon, head to the Dudhsagar Waterfalls, which is one of the highest waterfalls in India, and enjoy a refreshing dip in the cool waters. You can also try some adventure activities like zip-lining, rappelling, and trekking in the nearby hills.

Day 3: Island Tour and Departure

On your last day, take a boat ride to the nearby islands of Grand Island or Divar Island. You can explore the islands, go snorkeling or scuba diving, and enjoy a seafood lunch. In the evening, head back to Goa and catch your flight or train back home.

With its beaches, scenic beauty, and rich cultural heritage, a 3-day trip to Goa is sure to leave you refreshed and rejuvenated.
