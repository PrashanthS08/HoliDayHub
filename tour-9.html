GREENLAND

A 3-Day Trip to Greenland

Day 1: Kangerlussuaq and Northern Lights

On your first day, arrive in Kangerlussuaq and check into your hotel. In the evening, head out on a Northern Lights tour to witness one of the most stunning natural phenomena in the world. With its remote location and clear skies, Greenland is a perfect place to witness the aurora borealis.

Day 2: Ilulissat Icefjord and Dog Sledding

On your second day, take a day trip to the Ilulissat Icefjord, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a stunning example of natural beauty. Here, you can take a boat tour to get up close to the massive glaciers and icebergs, and learn about the local Inuit culture and traditions. In the afternoon, try dog sledding, which is a popular activity in Greenland and a fun way to explore the snowy landscapes.

Day 3: Nuuk and Departure

On your last day, take a flight to Nuuk, which is the capital of Greenland and a charming city with a rich history and culture. Visit the Nuuk Art Museum, which showcases the works of local artists and the history of Greenlandic art. In the afternoon, head back to Kangerlussuaq and catch your flight back home.

A 3-day trip to Greenland is a perfect way to experience the unique culture and stunning natural beauty of this remote and beautiful country.
