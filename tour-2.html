MYSURU

A 3-Day Trip to Mysuru

Day 1: Arrival and City Exploration

On your first day, arrive in Mysuru and check into your hotel. After freshening up, head to the Mysuru Palace, which is one of the most famous tourist attractions in the city. Here, you can explore the ornate architecture and beautiful interiors of the palace. You can also visit the nearby Jaganmohan Palace and Art Gallery, which houses a collection of paintings and artifacts. In the evening, take a stroll through the Devaraja Market, which is known for its colorful stalls and local produce.

Day 2: Local Sightseeing and Nature Exploration

On your second day, explore the local attractions of Mysuru. Visit the Chamundeshwari Temple, which is located on top of the Chamundi Hills and offers panoramic views of the city. You can also visit the Brindavan Gardens, which is a beautiful park with fountains and musical shows. In the afternoon, head to the nearby Ranganathittu Bird Sanctuary, which is home to a variety of migratory birds and offers boat rides along the river. You can also visit the nearby Srirangapatna, which is a historic town known for its temples and monuments.

Day 3: Day Trip to Coorg and Departure

On your last day, take a day trip to Coorg, which is a scenic hill station located about 2 hours from Mysuru. Here, you can visit the Abbey Falls, which is a beautiful waterfall surrounded by lush greenery. You can also visit the Dubare Elephant Camp, where you can interact with the elephants and take a bath with them in the river. In the afternoon, head back to Mysuru and catch your flight or train back home.

A 3-day trip to Mysuru is a great way to explore the rich culture and heritage of Karnataka, while also enjoying the natural beauty and scenic landscapes of the region.
