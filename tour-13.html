MURUDESHWARA

A 3-Day Trip to Murudeshwara

Day 1: Arrival and Beach Exploration

On your first day, arrive at Murudeshwara and check into your hotel. After freshening up, head to the Murudeshwara beach, which is known for its serene beauty and clean waters. You can take a dip in the sea, sunbathe on the shore, or simply relax and enjoy the stunning view of the Arabian Sea. In the evening, you can visit the famous Murudeshwara Temple, which is dedicated to Lord Shiva and has a 123-feet-tall statue of Lord Shiva. The temple also has a beautiful garden and a museum that showcases the history of the temple and the region.

Day 2: Local Sightseeing

On your second day, you can explore the local attractions of Murudeshwara. Visit the Netrani Island, which is a popular destination for snorkeling and scuba diving. You can also visit the Anegudde Vinayaka Temple, which is a famous temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, located about 30 km away from Murudeshwara. Another must-visit attraction is the Jog Falls, which is one of the highest waterfalls in India and is located about 80 km away from Murudeshwara.

Day 3: Outdoor Activities

On your last day, you can indulge in outdoor activities and explore the natural beauty of the region. You can go trekking to the nearby hills, which offer stunning views of the surrounding landscape. You can also visit the nearby wildlife sanctuaries, such as the Sharavathi Valley Wildlife Sanctuary, which is home to many species of animals and birds. In the evening, you can take a stroll along the beach and enjoy the beautiful sunset over the Arabian Sea.

On day 4, you can check out of your hotel and head back home, or continue your travels to other nearby destinations.
