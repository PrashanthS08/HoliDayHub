PONDICHERRY

A 3-Day Trip to Pondicherry

Day 1: Arrive in Pondicherry and Explore White Town

On your first day, arrive in Pondicherry and check into your hotel. Head out to White Town, which is the French Quarter of Pondicherry and a popular tourist destination. Visit the Sri Aurobindo Ashram, which is a spiritual center founded by Sri Aurobindo and the Mother, and explore the Auroville, which is an experimental township. In the evening, explore the local markets and try some of the delicious French and Tamil cuisine.

Day 2: Auroville and Beaches

On your second day, take a trip to Auroville, which is an experimental township and a unique community that aims to create a sustainable way of living. Visit the Matrimandir, which is a golden sphere surrounded by gardens and water bodies, and learn about the principles of Auroville. In the afternoon, head to the beaches of Pondicherry, such as Promenade Beach, Paradise Beach, or Auroville Beach, and relax in the serene atmosphere.

Day 3: Pondicherry Museum and Departure

On your last day, visit the Pondicherry Museum, which showcases the rich cultural heritage of Pondicherry. Explore the galleries that display artifacts from the Chola and Pallava dynasties, the French colonial era, and the local arts and crafts. In the afternoon, head back to your hotel and catch your flight back home.

A 3-day trip to Pondicherry is a perfect way to experience the unique culture, spiritual centers, and serene beaches of this charming town in Tamil Nadu, India.
